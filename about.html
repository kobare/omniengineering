About Company

Omni Engineering

Welcome to Omni Engineering! Your premier destination for experienced grinding, polishing and crankshaft repair services. We have been providing on-site crankshaft grinding repair services worldwide for over four decades. We aim to deliver the highest quality on-site crankshaft grinding and in-situ crankshaftgrinding for repair services to ship owners, power plants and industrial plants in a cost-effective manner. Our team of experienced technicians and engineers is always ready to serve in case of an emergency breakdown.

We cater to clients from various industries, including marine, power generation, steel plants, fertilizer plants, construction, aerospace, etc. Our team regularly travels worlwide with the latest and most portable on-site grinding equipment required for the repair process.
